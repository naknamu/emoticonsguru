What is an emoticon?

An emoticon is a representation of a facial expression something like this :-) (represents a smile), using keyboard characters to express the writer's emotions.

What is a textface (text face/text faces) or Lenny faces?

A textface (text face/text faces) or Lenny faces was named and popularized on 4chan which is used mostly for mischief or to imply hidden meaning behind a sentence.
